CLAUDE'S CONTRIBUTIONS
Everything else: the data architecture; the visual design and color system; the canvas-based lineage chart rendering engine; the origins map with D3.js; the Compare tab with F1/F2/BX prediction; the Breeder's Playground scoring engine and UI; the reverse lineage search with ancestry filters; the saved breeding projects system; the map zoom and pan; the column visibility toggles; the focus dimming effect; the availability system; and the research, writing, and verification of all 234 strain entries across 40 geographic regions. DISCLAIMER
Cannabis genetics records are frequently incomplete, contested, and sometimes deliberately obscured by breeders protecting proprietary lines. Lineages marked with ! are formally disputed within this dataset. All other lineages reflect the best available published information as of mid-2025 but should not be treated as authoritative or definitive. This is a research and exploration tool, not an official record.
