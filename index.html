HUMAN CONTRIBUTIONS
The owner's contributions were: the original idea to build a cannabis genetics visualization; the concept for the Breeder's Playground feature; the idea for the focus dimming effect on the lineage chart; the concept for the origins map detail panel with grow data; the idea for the light spectrum visualization; the idea for The Vault deep archive section; the idea for the persistent right-side strain detail panel; ongoing feedback and editorial direction throughout development; content priority decisions (which strains, seed banks, and eras to focus on); and the judgment calls about attribution, honesty, and what to launch publicly.
CLAUDE'S CONTRIBUTIONS
Everything else: the data architecture; the visual design and color system; the canvas-based lineage chart rendering engine; the origins map with D3.js; the Compare tab with F1/F2/BX prediction; the Breeder's Playground scoring engine and UI; the reverse lineage search with ancestry filters and quick-add presets; the saved breeding projects system; the map zoom and pan; the column visibility toggles with strain counts; the focus dimming effect; the availability system; the region detail panel with agronomic grow data, light spectrum charts, and personal notes; the sub-pin zoom system for specific origin locations; The Vault deep archive tab with collapsible cards, sort controls, S/M/L text sizing, and the persistent strain detail panel with cannabinoid visualisation and terpene descriptors; and the research, writing, and verification of all 258 main-canvas strain entries plus 250 Vault entries (508 total) across 40 geographic regions. Claude also proposed the map zoom feature, the column toggle system, The Vault, and the split detail-panel layout.
This project is an honest example of what human–AI collaboration looks like when the human brings domain curiosity, creative direction, and editorial judgment, and the AI brings research, implementation, and the majority of the ideation.

DISCLAIMER
Cannabis genetics records are frequently incomplete, contested, and sometimes deliberately obscured by breeders protecting proprietary lines. Lineages marked with ! are formally disputed within this dataset. All other lineages reflect the best available published information as of mid-2025 but should not be treated as authoritative or definitive. This is a research and exploration tool, not an official record.
